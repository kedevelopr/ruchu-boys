Brief History

Ruchu Boys High School, located in the heart of Kandara, Murang’a County, has long stood as a beacon of academic excellence and character formation in Central Kenya. Since its inception, the school has remained dedicated to nurturing disciplined, visionary young men. With a strong foundation in both spiritual and academic growth, Ruchu Boys has evolved into a premier institution known for its consistent performance in national examinations and its vibrant presence in co-curricular activities.
