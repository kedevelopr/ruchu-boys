Principal's
Official
Remarks

Welcome to the official website of Ruchu Boys High School.

Extra-County institution situated in Kandara Sub-County, our school carries a proud legacy of missionary excellence within the heart of Murang’a County. Our mission transcends mere academic instruction; we are firmly dedicated to the holistic 'molding of men,' ensuring that every student who passes through our gates emerges as a leader of integrity and substance.

Ruchu Boys stands as a beacon of discipline and hard work, providing a serene and focused environment where the young men of this great county are challenged to reach their full potential. We take pride in our commitment to the CBC Pathways—including STEM, Social Sciences, and Arts & Applied Sciences—as we prepare our students to thrive in a dynamic world.

"Excellence is our tradition, and character is our strength."
